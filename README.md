# Paper Trail
A warioware style microgame that's paper themed!

<img width="297" height="230" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/541fbd32-c22d-4906-91ef-bbb32c9630ae" />

### How to play:
You have 3 lives (displayed as rolls of tape on the top of the screen in between games). your goal is to complete 2 minigames before the time runs out.
Requires keyboard and mouse.
#### Game 1 - Platformer
In this game, you need to collect all the staplers. move with left and right, and jump with space. Try to find the fastest route 
#### Game 2 - Clicker
You need to click all the staplers before time runs out. This requires fast movement of the mouse

  <h5>The Bad Ending</h5>
  <p>You get this if you run out of lives at any of the levels. The screen says game over and there is a dead piece of paper at the bottom.</p>
  <h5>The Good Ending</h5>
  <p>You get this by beating both levels, and it results in the paper being surrounded by a heavenly light</p>
